Pool Safety Barrier Inspection

A thorough 'on site' inspection will be carried out by our Pool Safety Inspector which will assess your barrier against the requirement of the applicable Australian Standard.

Our qualified inspector will check your pool safety barrier including fencing, gates, windows, retaining walls, boundary fences and any other item of a safety barrier to prevent a young person accessing the pool area.

The outcome will be documented in a detailed electronic report.

  • Compliance assessment
  • Safety barrier remedy advice
  • Detailed electronic report
  • Certificate of Barrier Compliance

Re-inspection

Our inspector will re-inspect the barrier to determine if the non-compliance issues identified in the original inspection have been repaired and whether the barrier has been made compliant or not.

If the barrier remains non-compliant but progress has been made by the owner to make the barrier compliant, the inspector may provide a further period up to 7 days, prior to a further re-inspection required (additional cost).

We will issue you a Certificate of Compliance once your pool barrier complies with the Australian Standard.

  • Qualified re-inspection of your pool barrier
  • Certificate of Compliance if your pool safety barrier is compliant
  • Piece of mind for you!
