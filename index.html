Pool Safety Check

A thorough 'on site' inspection will be carried out by our Pool Safety Inspector which will assess your barrier against the requirement of the applicable Australian Standard.

This will determine compliance or non-compliance of the barrier.

  • Safety check of your pool
  • Compliance assessment
  • Adjustment advice if necessary

Barrier Inspection

Our qualified inspector will check your pool safety barrier including fencing, gates, windows, retaining walls, boundary fences and any items that are climbable.

This will be documented in a detailed electronic report.

  • Safety barrier check
  • Barrier adjustment advice
  • Electronic report

Certificates

A professional 'on site' inspection will be conducted by a registered, licensed and insured inspector.

Compliance Certificate will be issued if barrier complies.

  • On-site inspection
  • Assessment against applicable Australian standards
  • Certificate of Barrier Compliance

Consultation

We are more than happy to talk to you to advice you how to get your pool compliant.

Our consultation is free of charge!

  • Qualified and experienced inspector
  • Pre-inspection advice on the specifics of your pool
  • No extra charge!

Pre-sale Inspection

Make sure that your pool's certificate is up-to date and compliant!

Quick, qualified pre-house sale inspection.

  • Pre-sale compliance check
  • Up-to date certificate if required
  • Pre-sale piece of mind
